Privacy Policy

AI Form Copilot - Insurance Edition

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Overview

AI Form Copilot ("the Extension") is a browser extension developed by E&L Insurance Services, Inc. for internal use by insurance professionals. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles data.

Data Collection

What We DON'T Collect

  • The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • The Extension does not track browsing history
  • The Extension does not use analytics or tracking cookies
  • The Extension does not sell or share any data with third parties

What the Extension Accesses

The Extension accesses:

  1. Carrier Websites: Only when you click "Fill FAO" or "Fill GEICO" buttons
  2. Your Agency's CRM Server: To fetch client data for form filling
  3. Form Fields: To automatically fill insurance quote forms

Data Flow

  1. You enter a submission ID or search for a client
  2. The Extension requests data from your agency's secure CRM server
  3. The CRM server authenticates and returns client/driver/vehicle data
  4. The Extension fills form fields on the carrier website
  5. No data is stored in the Extension after the browser tab is closed

Data Storage

Local Storage

The Extension stores only:

  • Your server URL (for connecting to your agency's CRM)
  • Your name and email (for identification within your team)
  • Temporary session data (cleared when you close the browser)

No Cloud Storage

The Extension does not store any client, driver, or vehicle information in the cloud or on any external servers beyond your agency's CRM.

Third-Party Services

The Extension connects to:

  1. Your Agency's CRM Server - Managed by your agency administrator
  2. Insurance Carrier Websites - Progressive FAO, GEICO (only when filling forms)

The Extension does NOT connect to:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Any other third-party services

Security

  • All communication with your CRM server uses HTTPS encryption
  • CRM authentication credentials are stored server-side, never in the browser
  • The Extension only activates when you explicitly click buttons

Permissions Explained

Permission Why It's Needed
activeTab To fill form fields on the current carrier website
storage To save your server URL and preferences locally
scripting To inject the form-filling script into carrier pages
host_permissions To work on any carrier website

Children's Privacy

This Extension is intended for professional use by licensed insurance agents and is not designed for use by children under 13.
